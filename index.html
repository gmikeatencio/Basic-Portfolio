About Me

Originally born in Colorado, after graduting high school I decided to join the armed forces and start a career with the United States Marine Corps. I was trained in electrical avionics and worked on Ch-53 Echo helicopters. I had three tours overseas. After being honorablly discahrged I came to Austin, Texas to further my education at the University of Texas. From there I started a career at Samsung Semiconductor working as an Equipment Engineer. I am currently working towards a web design certificate and pursue a career as a full stack web developer.
